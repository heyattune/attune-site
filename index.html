How it works

From input to perfect song
in seconds

No streaming account needed. No sign-up required. Just tell Attune what you want.

1
Choose your mode
Search by song, describe a mood, or paste a Spotify playlist URL
2
Claude listens
Our AI analyses the sonic DNA, emotional tone, and cultural context
3
Discover music
Get 5 hand-picked recommendations with reasons — not just metadata matches
4
Preview & save
Hear 30-second previews right in the app before opening in your player

Every mode

However you discover,
we've got a mode for it

🎵
Song Mode
Type any song. Attune finds 5 tracks with a similar feel, vibe, and emotional resonance — not just the same genre.
Try: "Hotline Bling by Drake"
🎭
Mood Mode
Describe how you feel or the atmosphere you want. Claude translates your vibe into songs that match it perfectly.
Try: "melancholic, raining outside, reading"
🎧
Playlist Mode
Paste a public Spotify playlist. Claude analyses the collective vibe and recommends more songs that fit seamlessly.
Try: your favourite Spotify playlist URL
📅
Daily Vibe
Log your mood each day. Attune spots your patterns and generates a personalised playlist for how you typically feel on any given day.
Try: checking in every morning
👥
Friends
Each day, pin one song you're really into. See what your friends are listening to in a live feed — music discovery goes social.
Try: pinning today's earworm
✦
Why it's different
Spotify's algorithm optimises for streams. Claude optimises for you. Every recommendation comes with a reason — so you understand why, not just what.
No black box. Transparent AI.
