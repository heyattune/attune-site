Request removal of your Attune account and all associated personal data.

This action is permanent. Deleting your account removes your profile, journal entries, search history, and all personal data from our systems. This cannot be undone.

How to request deletion

1

Send us an email

Email us at heyattune@gmail.com with the subject line Delete my Attune account.

2

Include your account email

In your email, include the email address you used to sign up for Attune so we can locate your account.

3

We'll confirm within 7 days

We'll process your request and send a confirmation email once your account and data have been permanently deleted.
